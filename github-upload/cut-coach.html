Cut Coach.

a measurement mic + a laptop, turned into a chatter advisor

Point the iMM-6C at the cut and press listen. Cut Coach tells you whether you are chattering, gives the spindle speed to fix it, and remembers what runs clean on your machine. No tap test, no accelerometer, no modal numbers to fill in.

Setup

Set the spindle speed and depth you are actually running. The depth is only used to log this cut on your machine map. Turn off any voice or noise reduction is handled for you.

The fix

Listening will tell you if a speed change is worth it.

Cut fingerprint

One full circle is one spindle turn. A clean cut traces the same shape every turn (a tight flower). Chatter does not repeat, so it smears into a ring.

Your machine map

Every cut you log is one dot at its speed and depth, coloured by how it ran. Over a few dozen cuts this becomes the real stable map of your machine and tooling, the thing a generic lobe chart can never give you.
Cut Coach reads audible sound only. It finds chatter and the speed to escape it, and trends your own results. It does not measure tool wear or absolute stability limits. Built on the validated pocket-speed rule n = 60·fc/(z·k) and once-per-revolution synchronous analysis.
