Workflow: 1 tap test fills M1/M2 → 2 lobes shows the pockets → run one → 3 record the cut in Tune, pick the real in-cut peak, Picked → M1 fₙ refines the map → 4 the library mines what you saved. Every WAV carries its full context (shared inputs + operation info + analysis snapshot) in a metadata chunk; the file stays a normal playable WAV. One mic owner at a time. Recordings cap at 3 minutes. Lobes engine: zero order frequency domain, multi mode, equal or differential pitch; out of envelope: low speed slotting (process damping), ae under 5 percent of Ø.
