What actually moves these numbers
hex is king. Sandvik: max chip thickness is the most important parameter. hex=fz·sinκr, κr=cos−¹(1−2ae/D). At low ae the chip thins, so raise fz to hold hex.
Force & bending: D, z, ae, ap, fz, material, stickout L. δ ∝ L³.
Helix: higher helix lowers radial force/deflection and raises axial Fa (pull-out) — choose the holder accordingly.
rpm: sets Vc, Vf, power and the regeneration phase; it does not change static force.
