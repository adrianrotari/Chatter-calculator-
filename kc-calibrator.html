Chatter.calc · kc Calibrator

Back out kc1.1 and mc from two cuts on a known material, using spindle power. Same Kienzle pair the Lobes and Chip·Force pages use. Make both cuts on the same tool and engagement, changing only the feed.

Method: cutting power Pc = (power in cut − idle) × η. Specific force kc = 6×107·Pc[kW] / (ap·ae·vf[mm/min]) at the cut's mean chip thickness hm. Two hm points give mc (slope) and kc1.1 (value at h=1mm). Kr (radial ratio) needs a radial-force or deflection measurement; keep the material default for now.
